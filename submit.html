Submission Guidelines for Meretricious Musings

Overview:

We welcome submissions of poetry, prose, essays/letters, art, and photography. Our aim is to showcase diverse voices and perspectives, celebrating creativity in its many forms. Please read the following guidelines carefully before submitting your work. General Guidelines: 1. Submission Limit: You may submit up to 5 pieces per category during each reading period.
2. Reading Periods:We only accept submissions during our designated reading periods. Submissions outside these periods will not be considered.
3. Original Work: All submissions must be original and previously unpublished. Simultaneous submissions are accepted, but please notify us immediately if your work is accepted elsewhere.
4. Format:All written submissions should be in a readable font (e.g., Times New Roman, 12 pt), double-spaced, and submitted in .doc, .docx, or .pdf format. Art and photography should be submitted as high-resolution .jpeg or .png files.

Category-Specific Guidelines:

Poetry: - Submit up to 5 poems per reading period. - Each poem should be a maximum of 2 pages. - Include all poems in a single document with each poem starting on a new page.
Prose: - Submit up to 5 prose pieces per reading period. - Each piece should be no longer than 3,000 words. - Acceptable forms include short stories, flash fiction, and creative non-fiction.
Essays/Letters: - Submit up to 5 essays or letters per reading period. - Each piece should be no longer than 2,500 words. - We are interested in personal essays, reflective pieces, and open letters on a variety of topics.
Art: - Submit up to 5 pieces of art per reading period. - Include a brief description or artist statement (up to 100 words) for each piece. - Ensure files are high-resolution and clearly labeled with your name and title of the work.
Photography: - Submit up to 5 photographs per reading period. - Include a brief description or photographer statement (up to 100 words) for each photograph. - Ensure files are high-resolution and clearly labeled with your name and title of the work.
How to Submit: - All submissions should be sent via email to alexishassan999@gmail.com. - The subject line should read: "[Category] Submission: [Your Name]" - In the body of the email, include a brief bio (up to 100 words) and any relevant contact information. - Attach your submission(s) to the email in the appropriate format.
Response Time: - We aim to respond to all submissions within 8-12 weeks after the close of the reading period. If you have not heard from us by then, feel free to follow up. Rights: - By submitting, you grant Meretricious Musings first publication rights. All other rights revert to the author/artist upon publication. Should you choose to republish the work elsewhere, please acknowledge its initial publication with us. Thank you for considering Mereticious Musings for your work. We look forward to reading your submissions!
